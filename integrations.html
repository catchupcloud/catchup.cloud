At CatchUp, our focus is simplifying your work, and hence, providing a hassle-free pathway to connect to those numerous tools out there is a critical feature itself - and this is where the CatcUp-Zapier connect comes into play.

Zapier allows you to construct your own one-way communications called “Zaps” between any two apps available on its platform’s. Zapier also comes with a free plan to get you started.

Simplifying your work with ready-to-use Zaps

With CatchUp, we believe in providing excellence, so we went a step ahead to provide some done-for-you stuff, so you don’t have to! CatchUp offers several ready-to-use Zapier templates for some of the most popular Zaps. You just need to customize those for your data and you’re good to go!

Choose from over 2,000 integrations

The CatchUp Zapier Integration lets you connect CatchUp to 2,000+ other web services. Automated connections called Zaps, set up in minutes with no coding, can automate your day-to-day tasks and build workflows between apps that otherwise wouldn't be possible.

Each Zap has one app as the "Trigger", where your information comes from and which causes one or more "Actions" in other apps, where your data gets sent automatically.

How do I connect CatchUp to Zapier?

1. Log in to your Zapier account or create a new account.
2. Navigate to "My Apps" from the top menu bar.
3. Now click on "Connect a new account..." and search for "CatchUp"
4. Use your credentials to connect your CatchUp account to Zapier.
5. Once that's done you can start creating an automation! Use a pre-made Zap or create your own with the Zap Editor. Creating a Zap requires no coding knowledge and you'll be walked step-by-step through the setup.
6. Need inspiration? See everything that's possible with CatchUp and Zapier.
