Task Management

CatchUp focuses on short-term tasks that require coordination. Tasks can be filtered by user (creator, assignee or follower), client, project, matter and tags.

Real-time Communication

Tasks have a set of notes organized in a chronological order as a running timeline like a chat. Attachments can be added to the tasks. You can provide immediate feedback, ask questions, request updates for tasks and add fee or expense items.

Document Management & Collaboration

Catchup features an enterprise grade document management system to create, share and collaborate on documents in real-time.

Calendar View

A calendar view of all tasks is shown with their final deadline and where the current marker of completion is.

Wiki

You can share knowledge and unleash collective intelligence for your business through wiki and bulletin boards.

Video Conferencing

Enterprise-grade video conferencing and online meetings capability are available within CatchUp.

Provides Context & Content

CatchUp provides context and content as mapped to business and formal relationships driven by relationships. It allows real-time interaction, collaboration facilitation, and memorializes and facilitates all work product in a manner that it can be delivered to the client, customer or end consumer, and also allows linkage to resources for cost and resource efficiency, while allowing efficient execution of business processes in a distributed context.

Kanban & Action Items

CatchUp provides a Kanban view for action items. Action items can be created in multiple ways: (1) Directly from the Kanban page, (2) Mentioning users in Task Notes or Messages, (3) Mentioning users in document annotations.

Notes & To-do Lists

Manage your notes and to-do lists within CatchUp.

Light and Dark Themes

CatchUp has support for light and dark themes.

Time Tracking & Invoicing

Within CatchUp, you can track the time spent by users on different tasks, set a project budget, set billable rates for users, and generate invoices.

Secure & Customizable

CatchUp is a highly secure and customizable platform with the option of on-premises or cloud based deployment, and user interfaces optimized for all types of devices.
